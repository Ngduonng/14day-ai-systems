Refund Policy (Effective 2024)

1. General Refunds:
Items can be returned for a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided they are in their original condition and packaging. To initiate a refund, please contact customer support with your order number.

2. Software Subscriptions:
For annual subscriptions, refunds are prorated based on the remaining months. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be canceled at any time to prevent future billing.

3. Exceptional Circumstances:
If a product arrives damaged or defective, please notify us within 7 days of receipt. In such cases, a replacement or full refund will be provided regardless of the general 30-day policy.

4. Processing Time:
Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to process and appear on your original payment method. Contact us if you do not see your refund after 10 days.
